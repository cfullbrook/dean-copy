ABOUT THE BAND

The Wall of Floyd gives you a performance that provides the true essence of a Pink Floyd show from one of the greatest bands of all time.

With great musicians and a fabulous sound system, The Wall of Floyd delivers a great aural and visual experience with the quality you would expect from Pink Floyd themselves.

You’ll hear the well-known great tunes - The Wall, Shine On You Crazy Diamond, Money etc - along with a couple of the famous Syd Barrett tunes that started Pink Floyd on its amazing journey. Plus a couple of great songs from the Final Cut album, rarely played by other tribute bands.

The musicians in the 8-piece band have diverse backgrounds and ages, with the common thread that they all love the music and can reproduce it accurately.

Pink Floyd’s music appeals to a huge age range, from around 12yrs to 85yrs, and attracts thousands of new fans every year to its existing millions around the world.
